REDEEM - GET THOSE POINTS

We set out to develop an app that would offers you great rewards.

LOGO & COLORS

Our supervisor Ja Arce wanted that our logo should be simple or minimalist yet aesthetic and easy to recognize.

The combination of dark and light green gave us a strong feeling that people is ready to scan and redeem points.

PURCHASING

Once the user decided what to choose or to purchase from the available list of items the user will automatically gain points.

Points can be spent on prizes, promos and discounts.

DETECT STORES OFFER

Whenever the user is nearby at a local store that is registered from the system then the user will get what the stores offer.

The detection for local stores runs through Google Maps API and Django framework
